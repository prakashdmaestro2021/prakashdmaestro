About Misty Dews

Welcome to Misty Dews, your perfect getaway in the serene heart of Vakery, Wayanad, Kerala.

Whether you’re here for a peaceful retreat, an adventurous escape, or a cultural experience, our homestay offers a blend of comfort, hospitality, and local charm.

home

Stay Options

We offer a range of accommodation options to suit your needs: 

Room: Spacious with a king-sized bed, bathroom, and balcony views.

Family Suite: Perfect for groups, with three bedrooms and a shared living area.

Budget-Friendly Rooms: Cozy and comfortable, ideal for solo travelers.

Explore with Misty Dews

24/7 assistance for your convenience. Parking space for guests. Tranquil Surroundings: Unwind in our lush garden or enjoy the picturesque views from our rooftop terrace. A short distance away, offering stunning views and rich history. Dive into the local culture with a visit to Edakkal Caves.
